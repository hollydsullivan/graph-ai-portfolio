About the Course

Are you copy-pasting text into ChatGPT but struggling to see how AI can substantially transform your work? In this 8-week course, you’ll learn to automate significant parts of your content creation, research, data analysis, and knowledge management workflows as you build AI solutions to boost productivity for yourself and your team.


Course Modules

Documents, Presentations & Websites

Generate high-quality content for reports, papers, presentations and websites. Learn how large language models (LLMs) work and how to get the most out of them.

Topics: LLM fundamentals, content generation, no-code websites, Markdown documents, AI presentations

Automated Data Analysis

Master AI tools for analyzing data, manipulating files, and creating automated reports. Use Julius, ChatGPT Code Interpreter and Windsurf to process datasets, visualize results, and generate reports quickly.

GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Learn AI techniques for literature search, text analysis, and knowledge synthesis. Use tools like Elicit & LiteRev for literature reviews, and deploy LLMs for text classification, sentiment analysis and summarization.

Topics: Elicit, Literev, text summarization, text classification, sentiment analysis, knowledge extraction

Custom GPTs and Chatbots

Build chatbots for research assistance, knowledge management, or specific task support. Explore RAG systems, API integrations, and Botpress workflows.

Topics: RAG systems, Custom GPT Builder, Botpress workflows, API integration, knowledge base connection

AI Tools for Workflow Automation

Automate repetitive tasks across various platforms and workflows. Configure systems with Make.com to reclaim hours of your workweek.

Topics: Lindy automations, Make.com workflows, GSheets integration, Gmail API, task orchestration

GenAI for Communication & Outreach

Use AI tools to create assets for research dissemination and professional communications. Design visuals and presentations for sharing findings, pitching ideas or building awareness.

Topics: SVG, HTML and pixel-based image generation, AI video

Capstone Project & Showcase

Design and implement an AI solution for a real-world scenario. Receive feedback, troubleshoot technical issues, and present your work to your peers.

Topics: Project scoping, workflow design, tool integration, presentations and feedback
